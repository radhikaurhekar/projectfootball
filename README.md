# ⚽ English Premier League Performance Analytics



##  Project Overview

This project investigates which performance metrics most strongly influence final league position in the English Premier League.

We analyze three key parameters:

- Shot Accuracy  
- Goal Conversion Rate  
- Disciplinary Record (Yellow and Red Cards)  

The objective is to determine how stromgly these metrics  predict team success over a full season.



---



##  Research Question

How do performance metrics shot accuracy, goal conversion rate, and discipline influence final league position?



---



##  Hypotheses

1. Shot accuracy increases probability of winning matches  

2. Shot accuracy predicts league position  

3. Disciplinary standards influence overall team performance  

4. Higher possession leads to higher goal conversion  

5. Goal conversion is a stronger predictor of league position than accuracy and discipline  



---



##  Methodology

- Data cleaning and preprocessing  

- Feature engineering (accuracy, conversion rate, discipline score)  

- League table reconstruction (points and position)  

- Exploratory data analysis  


---



##  Key Findings

- Goal conversion rate showed the strongest relationship with league position.  

- Shot accuracy influenced match-level outcomes but was less predictive at season level.  

- Disciplinary performance showed moderate but inconsistent impact.  



---



##  Business Recommendation

Clubs underperforming in league position despite strong shot accuracy should prioritize:

- Recruitment of high-efficiency strikers  

- Finishing-focused training  

- Shot selection optimization  

- Clubs should focus on improving disciplinary standards
